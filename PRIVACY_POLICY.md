# Privacy Policy for TTDownloader - TikTokio

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **TTDownloader - TikTokio** ("the App") handles your information when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience.

## 1. Data Collection
### 1.1 Personal Information
The App **does not collect, store, or share** any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account registration, email addresses, or phone numbers.

### 1.2 Non-Personal Data
The App may process basic technical data purely for functionality:
- **Device Information**: Model and OS version (standard Android functionality).
- **Download Metrics**: Aggregate data on app usage to improve performance (no identity tracking).

### 1.3 Media Storage
All videos and photos downloaded through the App are stored **locally on your device's gallery**. We do not upload your media to any servers or cloud storage.

## 2. Permissions
To function correctly, the App requires:
- **Internet Access**: To fetch video data from TikTok servers.
- **Storage Access**: To save files to your gallery (compatible with Android 13+ Media Store APIs).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App utilizes standard third-party services that may have their own privacy policies:
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and security.
- **RapidAPI**: Acts as a bridge to fetch public video metadata.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of any such data, we will delete it immediately.

## 5. User Responsibilities
Users are responsible for the content they download. Please respect the copyright and intellectual property rights of original content creators. **TTDownloader - TikTokio** is not responsible for any misuse of downloaded content.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting in the App or on our public policy page.
